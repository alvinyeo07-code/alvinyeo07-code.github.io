About

I'm based in Bukit Jalil, KL and I run Tan & Yeo — a legal firm my father started, that I took over and have been building since.

We focus on property law — SPAs, loan docs, tenancy agreements, the works. I'm not a lawyer myself, but I manage the business side and I'm the person agents and bankers actually talk to day-to-day.

I care a lot about being easy to reach, honest about timelines, and making sure every referral partner feels looked after. If that sounds like what you're looking for — let's talk.
